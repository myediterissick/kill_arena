# Arcade Ops — Survive the Invasion

Earth isn’t just under attack… it’s being **overrun**.

A battle-worn **veteran** is forced back into the fight when **zombies**, **monsters**, and **robots** invade and turn the world into a warzone. No backup. No safe zones. Just you, your instincts, and whatever firepower you can scavenge and upgrade.

**25 levels. 50 advancements. One mission: survive.**

Jump in for the fun.

---

## What to Expect
- **Wave-based combat** across **25 levels**
- **50 advancements** to power up your run your way
- A mix of **zombies, monsters, and robots** with escalating chaos
- **Boss fights** that will test your aim, timing, and nerves
- Weapons you can **unlock and upgrade** as you go

---

## Controls
- Move: **WASD** or **Arrow Keys**
- Look: **Mouse**
- Jump: **Space**
- Crouch: **Shift**
- Sprint: **Tab**
- Reload: **R**
- Swap weapons: **Y**
- Menu: **ESC**
- Camera view: **Option/Alt** (toggle perspectives)
